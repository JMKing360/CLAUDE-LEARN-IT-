KOORA: what is running you. What is holding you. Where you go next.

There is a kind of suffering that does not look like suffering. The work that carries your name is the work that does not get done. You do not have a productivity problem. You have a fidelity problem.

UNFINISHED is a sixty-item diagnostic taken every fifteen days across the 180-day path of KOORA. It maps six domains of self-fidelity, names the pattern most loud in you this period, and tracks how that pattern moves over time. The pattern is the diagnosis, never the verdict.

About fifteen minutes. Each return is evidence. Each return is the work.

The four reflexes · Pattern profile

How to read this: Each reflex scores 0 to 16. Quiet ran rarely. Active ran often enough to notice. Loud dominated this period. The arrow shows movement since your last return.

The Allegiance Matrix · How the loudest reflex broke each faculty

How to read this: The matrix shows the four reflexes against the four faculties of fidelity. The gold-bordered row is your loudest reflex this period. Below it, the fingerprint shows specifically how that reflex broke each faculty.

The seven postures · ALCARRA, the week as you lived it

How to read this: Each posture scores 1 to 4. Strong means the posture was alive in your week. Thin means it was barely present. ALCARRA names the seven daily commitments that compose a coherent week.

The four needs · Filled, or substituted

How to read this: Each need has two columns. Filled is how met the genuine need was. Substituted is how much you reached for a counterfeit. High filled with low substituted is health. The reverse is the warning.

The forces of necessity · What moved you

How to read this: The four forces are the obligations that move a Finisher from intention to motion. Duty is who you are honoring. Opus is what was given you to build. Declaration is what you put on record. Identity is who you must become.

The four fidelities · What you held this period

How to read this: The four fidelities test whether you kept what was yours to keep, across mind, heart, will, and hand. Each scores 1 to 4. The covenant of the week is sealed by these four together.

The four faculties of fidelity · Direct measure

How to read this: The four faculties of fidelity, named by the Allegiance Matrix, are the territories the reflexes break. This table measures each directly. Embodied is held. Beginning is the next place to grow.
