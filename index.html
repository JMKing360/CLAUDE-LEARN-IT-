    How is this scored?

    Each item uses a four-point Likert anchor: Almost never (1), Some weeks (2), Most weeks (3), This is how I live (4). The instrument has sixty items across six sections.

    The four reflexes (Complaining, Criticizing, Comparing, Competing) each score 4–16. We classify quiet at ≤44%, active at 45–69%, loud at 70%+. Reverse-scored items in ALCARRA, the four needs (filled), forces, fidelities, faculties, and the daily practice flip the scale so that high scores indicate embodied capacity rather than absence.

    The tier resolver selects which of the six covenant variants (Self · Body · Craft · People · Future · World) you see at each retake, mapped from your chosen day on the 180-day path. Day 0 is your baseline; Day 14/30 is the Self covenant; Day 45/60 is Body; and so on through to Day 180, the Sealing.

    The scoring methodology is observational - the pattern is the diagnosis, never the verdict.

    The Allegiance Matrix

    The seven postures · ALCARRA, the week as you lived it

    How to read this: Each posture scores 1 to 4. Strong means the posture was alive in your week. Thin means it was barely present. ALCARRA names the seven daily commitments that compose a coherent week.

    The four needs · Filled, or substituted

    Where filled is low and substituted is high, the substitute has been keeping you from the real thing.

    How to read this: Each need has two columns. Filled is how met the genuine need was. Substituted is how much you reached for a counterfeit. High filled with low substituted is health. The reverse is the warning.

    The forces of necessity · What moved you

    If two forces feel close, choose the heavier one. That is the active force for today.

    How to read this: The four forces are the obligations that move a Finisher from intention to motion. Duty is who you are honoring. Opus is what was given you to build. Declaration is what you put on record. Identity is who you must become.

    The four fidelities · What you held this period

    A covenant is sealed by these four together, not by any one of them alone.

    How to read this: The four fidelities test whether you kept what was yours to keep, across mind, heart, will, and hand. Each scores 1 to 4.

    The four faculties of fidelity · Direct measure

    These four are the territories the reflexes break. Their direct measure is the cleanest read of where return is asked.

    How to read this: The four faculties of fidelity, named by the Allegiance Matrix, are the territories the reflexes break. Embodied is held. Beginning is the next place to grow.
