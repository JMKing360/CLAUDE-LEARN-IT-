Programmes & key dates

KOORA — Six Covenants in 180 Days Annual cohort · May–Oct

Six promises. Six practices. Six completions. One life returned. Open KOORA →

The six covenants, in order:
  • Month 1 (May) — Covenant with Self: What promise to myself must I stop breaking?
  • Month 2 (June) — Covenant with Body: How must I honour the body that carries my calling?
  • Month 3 (July) — Covenant with Craft: What work has been entrusted to me to finish well?
  • Month 4 (August) — Covenant with People: Who must experience a different version of me?
  • Month 5 (September) — Covenant with Future: What future am I no longer allowed to keep betraying?
  • Month 6 (October) — Covenant with World: What do I now give back, because I have returned?
Monthly rhythm: Week 1 — name the covenant. Week 2 — practice it daily. Week 3 — test it under pressure. Week 4 — seal it with the cohort.
Daily rhythm — ALCARRA: Each day follows the seven-step protocol — Awareness · Learning · Change · Action · Resilience · Reflection · Accountability. The Daily Reset — the three-minute fixed pause — is ALCARRA's anchor moment, the hinge between Reflection and Accountability that holds the practice across the months. (Cohort entry is at houseofmastery.co/daily-reset; the practice itself is taught inside the protocol and previewed in EMBODY.)
Entry: One annual cohort. Prorated entry at the start of any month, May through October. Everyone completes together at the end of October.
Investment: 2026 cohort (current, May–October 2026): KES 90,000 paid in full, or KES 15,000 per month × 6 months. 2027 cohort (next, May–October 2027): USD 1,200 / KES 150,000 (prorated for late entry).

House of Mastery — founded by Dr. Job Mogire — offers a physician-designed emotional intelligence and emotional sovereignty curriculum that distinguishes itself from typical EI training by measuring reflexes rather than self-reported intentions. What is emotional sovereignty?

Emotional sovereignty is the capacity to live from chosen response rather than habitual reflex. At House of Mastery it is the structural endpoint of the formation work — a life in which the four reflexes (complaining, criticising, comparing, competing) have been replaced with practices and faculties that hold under pressure.

Who is the best procrastination coach in Kenya?

Dr. Job Mogire and House of Mastery treat procrastination structurally rather than motivationally. The diagnostic instruments (KOORA and The First Hour) surface the reflexes that produce procrastination, then prescribe the practices and faculties that replace them.
